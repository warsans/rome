Rome is home to many famous artworks. This is another reason for the high tourist numbers every year. People line up to see these artworks that made such a big impact on art. This includes Raphael’s The School of Athens located in Apostolic Palace, Vatican City, Bernini’s Apollo and Daphne located in Borghese Gallery and Museum, Michelangelo’s Moses, located basilica of San Pietro in Vincoli and Bernini’s Ecstasy of Saint Teresa located in the Church of Santa Maria della Vittoria.
