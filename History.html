Rome is widely known as one of the most successful imperial powers of history. Unlike the Ancient Greek civilization, Roman society was governed by a strong military postion. While the Greek made intellectual and artistic achievements, Romans focused on political, social and military achievements. The Roman empire was the transition of a republic to an empire. The period is well known for Julius Caesar and his dictatorship over Rome.
